We are Social


Ruder Finn has been family owned + operated since our founding in 1948. Being privately held gives us the freedom to let lose our imagination.
Established in 2011, Ruder Finn India has wholly owned offices across key Indian metros including New Delhi (Gurugram), Mumbai, Bangalore and Hyderabad.

Headquartered in India out of New Delhi, Ruder Finn has a strong nationwide network of affiliates reaching over 40 media hubs across the country. We connect to the rest of the world through our dual headquarters in the U.S. and China and have an extensive market presence across the US, Europe and Asia.

We thrive on helping businesses and institutions transform the way they do business, go to market and communicate with the people that matter most, leveraging technology as the disruptive force.
