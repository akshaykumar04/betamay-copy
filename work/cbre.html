CBRE

#WalkForAWish

Walk For A Wish is CBRE's flagship philanthropic initiative that supports the company’s commitment towards various social responsibilities by bringing in teams and communities together for a walk that raises awareness on several humanitarian issues. As part of its corporate social responsibility, CBRE will focus on providing support to the children/adults with special needs

In the previous editions, CBRE had enrolled two especially abled students with CBRE. With the fourth edition of ‘Walk For A Wish’ initiative, we had limited to no news to share with the media. The brief was to make this event successful by ensuring media presence at the event, followed by coverage across all the regional publications.

What we did

We highlighted that CBRE is working towards ensuring that these individuals are trained for employment in the corporate world and make them a part of the growing economy.

As part of our media outreach, we ensured a diverse mix of technology and real estate media from Gurgaon. On the sideline of the event, we not only scheduled interviews with regional media but, also aligned video interviews with technology bloggers on how the company is focused on creating an environment of inclusiveness, one that aligns with company’s ‘RISE Values’.

We were featured in prominent publications such as Dainik Bhaskar, Dainik Jagran, Punjab Kesari, Amar Ujala, Navbharat Times, along with other regional dailies helping us enhance our readership by manifolds
