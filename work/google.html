Shaping Future Market Considerations & Customer Buying Decisions through Analyst Relations

Two years ago, cloud buyers didn’t see Google as a credible enterprise player – certainly not compared to Amazon Web Services or Microsoft Azure. Google’s engineering and consumer heritage, which had made the company so formidable with some audiences, worked against them when it came to B2B and technology customers.

Our influencer work in Asia and the US has helped dramatically change that perception – at equally dramatic scale.

We did this by showing how Google’s cloud data and analytics transformed customers’ businesses and ways of working from the customers’ point of view, not just that of Google engineers, while also showcasing the ways Google was solving for some of the biggest societal problems on the planet.
